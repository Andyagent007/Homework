  2. Do you remember this interesting fact about the tangent function from trigonometry? Math.tan(a+b) == (Math.tan(a) + Math.tan(b)) / (1 - Math.tan(a) * Math.tan(b)). Use your skills with grade school math to evaluate Math.tan(x+y) — specifically, do not use the console. Show all the little steps of your solution.

    answer

    1. (Math.tan(x) + Math.tan(y)) / (1 - Math.tan(x) * Math.tan(y))
    2. (Math.tan(3/5) + Math.tan(15/7)) / (1 - Math.tan(3/5) * Math.tan(15/7))
    3. (96/35) / (1 - (3/5 * 15/7))
